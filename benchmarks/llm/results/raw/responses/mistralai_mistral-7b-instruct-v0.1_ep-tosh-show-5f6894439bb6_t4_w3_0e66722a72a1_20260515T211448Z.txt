 [1837.5s - 1861.3s] Ad segment:
This is an advertisement for Mint Mobile. The host promotes the idea of saving money by switching to Mint Mobile, using the phrase "Let's go" as a transition. The ad contains the promotional language "unfortunately, those big wireless carriers also seem to be keeping my money too" and "after years of overpaying for wireless, are you fed up with crazy high bills?" The call to action is implied, as the audience is encouraged to consider switching to Mint Mobile.
